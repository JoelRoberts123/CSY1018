Compasion of web dev jobs

On this page, i will comparing multiple web developer jobs up against each other. I will be using a site called https://www.cwjobs.co.uk/ to find the best comparable jobs to see how they stack up against one another

The first web developing job i have found is located in Maidstone, Kent and offers a competitive salary of £35,000 - £45,000. This job requires you to work for one of the most successful leaders in the foods industry. The job description states that as a member of the team, you will be required to write code for new applications bieng developed as w ell as maintain the existing products. The benefits of this apprenticeship include a competitive salary, an on site gym, free parking and an on site cafeteria. however you need required skills for this apprenticeship as well as experience in c++, MVC and Javascript.

The second job i have found is a web developer job in London that requires you to specialise in Javascript, The job description states that this successful online gaming firm is seeking a skilled Web End Developer to design and develop an interactive gaming product that deals with in excess of 6,000 messages every 15 minutes. Like the previous job, this job also has requirements. You must have strong Javascript skills as well as previous experience using Javascript and excellent communication skills as you are required to work as part of a team.Thid job offers a competitive salary of £55k a year, which is more than the previous job.

The final job i am looking at is for a software developer in Dunstable that offers a salary of 30k which is less than the other two job offers. This job also looks for experience as well as the other two jobs. This job looks for people who can specialise in Java and Angular as well as other traits such as strong problem solving skills and have the ability to work in a fast paced enviroment as well as have strong communication skills to attract customers as well as attract third party companies.
